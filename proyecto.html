Project

Mision

  • To make known the ways that people have to get success into a guide for personal growth.
  • To introduce the application of skills to get personal growth.
  • To understand how self-knowledge and ethics can release obstacles to the development of personal growth.

Vision

  • With this guide we hope to take a focus on the perception of success in people.
  • Manage a personal projection in each one, giving rise to the main characteristics of a good quality of life.
  • To be able to fit correctly into all the key concepts that correspond to personal success and that must be taken into account for a correct effectiveness of the planned objective.

Methodology

The methodology to be applied is based on the analysis of experiences, through the development of a guide in each of the people, which will allow immersion in the visualization and planning of personal objectives.

    Guide

    To achieve the proposed objective, a blog will be made where the guide containing the aspects that are considered important for personal growth will be visually displayed.

      Target audience

      The guide is primarily aimed at a large number of young people and adults who are interested in knowing the process to achieve personal success.
